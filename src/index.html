Air Quality Checker

How It works?

The application has the goal to verify the current air pollution state using data that have been kindly provided us by the World Air Quality Index Project.

You can check the current air pollution quality by either using your device location data or providing a location of your choice.
Please remember, the air pollution data displayed are always referencing the data provided by the nearest measuring station.
